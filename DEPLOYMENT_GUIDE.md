# Portfolio Deployment Guide

## Quick Deploy Options

### Option 1: Netlify (Recommended - Easiest)

1. **Go to Netlify**: https://www.netlify.com
2. **Sign up** for a free account (use GitHub, Google, or email)
3. **Drag and Drop**:
   - Go to your Netlify dashboard
   - Find the "Sites" section
   - Drag your entire project folder (with `vishu.html` and image files) into the deploy area
4. **Get your link**: Netlify will automatically generate a link like:
   - `https://random-name-12345.netlify.app`
5. **Customize domain** (optional): You can change it to something like `vishwajeet-portfolio.netlify.app` in site settings

**Your LinkedIn-ready link will be ready in seconds!**

---

### Option 2: GitHub Pages (Free & Professional)

1. **Create GitHub account**: https://github.com (if you don't have one)
2. **Create new repository**:
   - Click "New repository"
   - Name it: `portfolio` or `vishwajeet-portfolio`
   - Make it Public
   - Click "Create repository"
3. **Upload files**:
   - Click "uploading an existing file"
   - Upload `vishu.html` and your image file
   - Commit the changes
4. **Enable GitHub Pages**:
   - Go to Settings â†’ Pages
   - Under "Source", select "main" branch
   - Click Save
5. **Get your link**: Your site will be at:
   - `https://yourusername.github.io/portfolio`

---

### Option 3: Vercel (Fast & Modern)

1. **Go to Vercel**: https://vercel.com
2. **Sign up** (free with GitHub/Google)
3. **Import Project**:
   - Click "Add New" â†’ "Project"
   - Drag and drop your files
   - Click Deploy
4. **Get your link**: Instant deployment with a link like:
   - `https://your-project.vercel.app`
